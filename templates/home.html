About Novara

Novara is a powerful platform designed to connect users with a wide range of research and internship opportunities—quickly, efficiently, and at scale. Instead of spending hours crafting and sending individual emails, Novara automates the process by generating personalized outreach based on each user's profile and preferences. With just a few clicks, users can send hundreds of professionally tailored emails to potential mentors, labs, or institutions, saving valuable time while maximizing their chances of success. Our mission is to break down barriers to opportunity by leveraging smart automation, allowing users to focus more on growth and less on logistics.
